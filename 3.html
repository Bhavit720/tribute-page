VIRAT KOHLI - THE KING

Personal Life of Virat Kohli: A Glimpse Beyond the Cricket Field
  • Early Life and Family
    1. Born: November 5, 1988, in Delhi, India, to Prem Kohli (a criminal lawyer) and Saroj Kohli (a homemaker).
    2. Virat grew up with his elder brother, Vikash Kohli, and elder sister, Bhavna Kohli.
    3. A tragedy struck his family early when his father passed away in 2006, a moment that strengthened Kohli’s resolve to succeed in cricket.
  • Marriage and Family
    1. Virat Kohli married Bollywood actress Anushka Sharma on December 11, 2017, in a private ceremony in Tuscany, Italy. Their union is celebrated as one of India’s most admired power couples, often called "Virushka" by fans.
    2. On January 11, 2021, the couple welcomed their daughter, Vamika Kohli, bringing immense joy to their lives. They keep her away from the limelight, respecting her privacy.
  • Lifestyle and Interests
    1. Fitness Icon: Kohli is known for revolutionizing fitness standards in Indian cricket. His strict diet, workout routines, and discipline have inspired millions.
    2. Food Enthusiast: Though once a fan of butter chicken, Kohli switched to a plant-based diet in 2018 for health and performance reasons.
    3. Hobbies: Kohli enjoys playing video games, listening to music, and spending quality time with his family.
    4. Fashion Icon: Known for his impeccable style, he’s often seen making headlines for his fashion choices and owns the WROGN clothing brand.
