VIRAT KOHLI - THE KING

    Carrer

  • Early Life and Beginnings (1988-2008)
    1. Born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, Virat Kohli showcased a passion for cricket from a young age.
    2. Trained under Rajkumar Sharma at the West Delhi Cricket Academy, he quickly emerged as a prodigy.
    3. In 2008, Kohli captained the Indian U-19 team to victory in the ICC U-19 Cricket World Cup held in Malaysia. This achievement marked his entry onto the international stage.
  • Rise to Stardom: ODI Debut and Early Success (2008-2011)
    1. Kohli made his ODI debut against Sri Lanka in August 2008.
    2. He scored his maiden ODI century in 2009, cementing his spot in the team.
    3. A crucial part of India's 2011 ICC Cricket World Cup-winning squad, Kohli scored an essential 83 in the opening match and supported Gautam Gambhir in the final with a steady 35.
